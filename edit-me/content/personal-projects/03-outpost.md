[github.com/qiangli/outpost](https://github.com/qiangli/outpost)

Open-source home-host bridge that securely registers local machines with the DHNT portal and tunnels loopback apps, shells, desktops, clipboard, and model services to authenticated users. Designed for auditable self-hosting with cross-platform installers and source builds.

**Stack:** Go | Reverse tunnels | WebSocket | PTY | VNC | OAuth integration
