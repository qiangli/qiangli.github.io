[github.com/dhnt/cloudbox](https://github.com/dhnt/cloudbox) | live: [ai.dhnt.io](https://ai.dhnt.io/)

DHNT portal and asset hub that coordinates paired outposts, hosted apps, scoped tokens, skills, tools, models, vault secrets, and audit trails. Connects cloud identity with locally hosted developer environments while preserving a clean open-source infrastructure boundary.

**Stack:** Go | React | SQLite/Postgres | OAuth | GORM | Matrix tunnel
